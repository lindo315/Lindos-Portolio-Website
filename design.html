    STYLE GUIDE

    This is my Style Guide part, it is separate from the design seciton about for clearity and features more detailed design choices.

    Color Scheme


    • Background: The background of my website uses a linear gradient from white to #d1d7db (a more pale greyish color), creating a cool and neutral tone that is easy on the eyes.

    • Accents: To add some visual interest to my website, I've chosen to use bold accent colors throughout. My accent colors include #0F2027 (a dark blue) and #2C5364 (a lighter shade of blue)

    • HEX Values:

      • Background: #FFFFFF (white) to #d1d7db
      • Accents: #0F2027, #2C5364

    • Usage Guidelines:

      • Button: #2C5364
      • Links (Tabs): #0F2027
      • Headings: #000000 (black)

    Typography


    • Headings and subheadings: I've chosen to use a font stack that includes several sans-serif fonts: system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', and 'Helvetica Neue'. This font stack provides a clean and modern look for my headings and subheadings.

    • Body text: For the body text on my website, I've chosen to use Arial. This font is a classic choice for body text and is easy to read, making it a great choice for my website content.

    • Font Sizes:

      • Headings: 80px (H1), 70px (H2), 30px (H3)
      • Body Text: 20px

    • Line Heights:

      • Headings: 1.2em
      • Body Text: 1.5em
      • Subheadings: 1.3em

    Imagary


    • Photos: For this I chose to use high-quality photographs throughout the website to showcase our my art, webpage and wireframes, and enhance the overall user experience. I selected the right photos that go along with what I want the website to convey to others.

    • Illustrations: In addition to photographs, I chose to use a few illustrations on some of some of the sections to add visual interest and break up text-heavy content. These illustrations (lines) were costume designed by me to fit the structuring of my wesite.

    • Image Sizes: To ensure that my images look great, I've optimized their sizes. The photos are sized to fit any div they are under and range from 1000px in width to 800px - 250px, and 500px - 250px in height. They are sized appropriately for their placement on the page.

    • Image Formats: I've chosen to use a JPEG format for my images, with transparent backgrounds where applicable. This is for consistancy if I choose to reuse code for uploading different pictures on the same size/dimensions.

    Layout


    • Grid System: To create a consistent and organized layout throughout my website, I've implemented a grid system in my work section. The grid consists of 2 columns and 3 rows, allowing me to showcase my weekly work in an organized and visually appealing way. Each column has a maximum width of 510px and each row has a maximum height of 300px, ensuring that my content is easily visible and accessible.

    • White Space: I've also incorporated ample white space into my layout to help content breathe and create a balanced composition. This not only makes the website more visually appealing but also improves readability and user experience.

    • Responsive Design: NOT YET AVAILABLE.

    • Navigation: To improve user experience and help users find what they're looking for, I've implemented a clear and easy-to-use navigation system. The navigation is located at the top of the page (homepage), with dropdown menus where applicable.

    My Goal for this Website

    My goal is to create a modern and dynamic portfolio website that showcases my weekly (school work) work and portfolio work outside of school in an organized and visually appealing way, while prioritizing readability and user experience. I aimed to achieve this through a clean and minimalist design, with a focus on bold typography, ample white space (where needed), and a color palette that is both sophisticated and eye-catching.


    This goal is aligned with my overall objective of growing my currenlty non-existant client base and expanding my reach in the industry. To ensure that the website is aligned with this goal, I've created a clean and professional (or so I think) design that highlights my current skills and work in a cool way (or so I think). I've also prioritized user experience, making it easy for potential "clients" to navigate my website and find the information they need. By aligning my website's goals with our overall business objectives, I can ensure that our online presence is a valuable asset in achieving me long-term goals.
